Important

  • These are NOT Sierra Club hikes — you are responsible for your own safety, medications, and first aid.
  • The leader is only there to share the route, not to be responsible for your safety or liability.
  • Most hikes start at 10AM, carpool at 9AM, unless otherwise noted. Check each hike for time and carpool.
  • Hike leader is not obliged to wait for latecomers nor to go to the carpool location.
  • Hike leader may cancel if fewer than 4 hikers or if heavy rain. Contact the hike leader if in doubt.
  • Some parks charge a parking fee — bring money.
  • Hike ranking code: Elevation change — A = up to 1000 ft, B = 1000–2000 ft, C > 2000 ft. Length — 1 = up to 6 mi, 2 = 6–10 mi, 3 = 10–15 mi.
  • ❖❖ indicates the hike is over 8 miles or strenuous — strong, experienced hikers only.
  • 🍔 indicates the hike includes an optional restaurant lunch; 🍝 indicates a gourmet potluck lunch.
  • ☎ indicates you must phone or contact the leader for a reservation by 8PM the night prior to the hike.

Rules of the Road

  • Bring food, drink, and plenty of water.
  • Bring extra water and salt/electrolytes on hot days.
  • Wear appropriate footwear.
  • No smoking, pets, or radios.
  • Plan to carpool at listed locations.
  • Pay drivers for transportation.
  • Don’t cut trails or pick plants.
  • Close gates and carry out refuse.
  • Inform hike leader of medical problems or if you contract COVID.
  • Carry with you emergency prescriptions and medical form.
  • Be sensitive to the needs of others and to the ecology.
  • Stay with the group at all times — sign out if leaving early, no singles.
